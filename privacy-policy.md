## Welcome to Listie

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

**The short version:** Listie stores your meal data locally on your device. We don't sell your data. We don't track you across apps. We don't spam you.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device
Listie stores all your core data **directly on your iPhone**:
- Your list of dishes (names, ingredients, recipe links)
- Your weekly meal plans
- Your grocery lists
- Your app settings (meal times, week start day, language)

**This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly delete and reinstall the app.**

### Data We Collect
When you use Listie, we may collect:

**Account Information:**
- Email address (only if you subscribe to Listie+)
- Subscription status (active, expired, trial)

**Usage Information:**
- App opens and session duration
- Feature usage (which screens you visit)
- Crash reports and error logs (anonymous)

**Device Information:**
- Device type (iPhone model)
- iOS version
- App version
- Language preference
- Timezone

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- Your actual meal names or ingredients
- Your grocery list contents
- Your recipe links
- Your photos
- Your location (we don't track where you eat)
- Your contacts
- Your browsing history outside Listie

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

### Local Data (On Your Device)
Your meals, plans, and groceries are **stored locally** and used only to:
- Display your weekly meal plans
- Generate your grocery lists
- Remember your preferences

### Data We Collect
We use collected data to:
- Process your Listie+ subscription (via Apple)
- Send important updates about your subscription (via Apple)
- Fix bugs and improve the app
- Understand which features people use (anonymous analytics)
- Comply with legal obligations

**We do NOT:**
- Sell your data to third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Share your data with data brokers
- Track you across other apps or websites

---

## 3. Data Sharing

### Third Parties We Work With

**Apple (In-App Purchases):**
When you subscribe to Listie+, Apple processes your payment. Apple shares minimal information with us:
- Subscription status (active/expired)
- Subscription start date
- No credit card details (Apple keeps those)

Read Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

**Analytics (Optional):**
We use anonymous analytics to understand app usage:
- No personal identifiers
- Aggregated data only
- You can opt out in iOS Settings → Privacy

**We do NOT share your data with:**
- Advertisers
- Data brokers
- Social networks
- Marketing companies

---

## 4. Data Security

### How We Protect Your Data

**Local Storage:**
Your meal data is encrypted using iOS native security:
- Secure keychain for sensitive settings
- Device-level encryption (iOS default)
- No cloud backup of sensitive data (unless you enable iCloud)

**Network Security:**
When we communicate with our servers (for subscriptions):
- HTTPS encryption (TLS 1.3)
- No plain-text transmission
- Minimal data sent

**Access Control:**
- Your data is protected by your iPhone passcode/Face ID/Touch ID
- We have no way to access your local meal data
- Only you can see your dishes and plans
