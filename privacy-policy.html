Privacy Policy

Welcome to Listie

Your privacy matters. This policy is simple because Listie is simple.

The short version: Listie stores everything locally on your device. We collect almost nothing. We don't sell your data. We don't track you. We don't spam you.

1. What Data Does Listie Collect?

Everything Stays on Your Device

Listie stores 100% of your personal data locally on your iPhone:

  • Your dishes (names, ingredients, recipe links)
  • Your weekly meal plans
  • Your grocery lists
  • Your app settings (meal times, week start day, language)

This data NEVER leaves your device.
We cannot see your meals. We cannot access your personal data.

What We Actually Collect: Almost Nothing

Before you subscribe:

  • No email
  • No name
  • No account
  • No tracking
  • No analytics
  • NOTHING personal

After you subscribe to Listie+:

  • Email address (shared by Apple, used only for subscription support)
  • Subscription status (active/expired, managed by Apple)
  • Transaction ID (to verify your subscription with Apple)

That's it. Really.

Anonymous Device Data (Anti-Abuse Protection)

To protect our free trial system from abuse and ensure fairness for all users, Listie collects and stores the following anonymous technical data:

  • Anonymous Device Identifier — a technical hardware ID, not linked to your identity, name, email, or Apple ID
  • Installation date — when you first installed Listie, used to enforce the 21-day free trial
  • Number of free meal plans used — a number between 0 and 3

This data is stored securely on Supabase servers located in the EU (GDPR compliant) and is used exclusively to:

  • Prevent unlimited free usage through repeated app reinstallation
  • Enforce the 21-day free trial and 3 free meal plan limits fairly

This data is:

✓ Completely anonymous — never linked to your name, email, or Apple ID
✓ Never used for tracking, advertising, or profiling
✓ Never shared with third parties
✓ Automatically deleted 90 days after your last app activity
✓ Stored on EU servers (GDPR compliant)

Why we collect this: Without this minimal protection, the free trial system could be bypassed indefinitely by uninstalling and reinstalling the app, which would make Listie unsustainable as a business.

What We Do NOT Collect (Ever)

  • Your meal names or ingredients
  • Your grocery lists
  • Your recipe links
  • Your photos
  • Your location
  • Your contacts
  • Your browsing history
  • Usage analytics
  • Device identifiers used for marketing or advertising

2. How We Use Your Data

Local Data (On Your Device)

Your meals, plans, and groceries are stored locally and used only to:

  • Display your weekly meal plans
  • Generate your grocery lists
  • Remember your preferences

We never see this data. It's on your device only.

Anonymous Device Data (Anti-Abuse)

Used solely to enforce the free trial limits fairly. Never used for any other purpose.

Subscription Data (From Apple)

When you subscribe to Listie+, Apple shares:

  • Your email (so we can provide support if needed)
  • Subscription status (so the app knows you're a subscriber)

We use this ONLY to:

  • Verify your subscription is valid
  • Send important subscription updates (via Apple)
  • Provide support if you contact us

We do NOT:

  • Sell your email to anyone
  • Send marketing emails
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Track you across apps or websites

3. Data Sharing

Apple (In-App Purchases)

When you subscribe, Apple processes your payment and shares minimal info with us:

  • Email address
  • Subscription status
  • Transaction ID

Apple's role:

  • Processes all payments
  • Manages your subscription
  • Handles refunds

Read Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Supabase (Anonymous Device Protection)

We use Supabase to store anonymous device data for free trial protection.

What Supabase stores:

  • Anonymous device identifier (no personal information)
  • Installation date (timestamp)
  • Number of free meal plans used (0 to 3)

Supabase details:

  • Servers located in the EU (GDPR compliant)
  • Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • No personal data is shared with Supabase
  • Supabase Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

Third Parties

We do NOT work with:

  • Advertisers
  • Analytics companies
  • Data brokers
  • Marketing platforms
  • Social networks

We don't share your personal data with anyone (except Apple for subscriptions and Supabase for anonymous trial enforcement).

4. Data Security

Your Local Data

Protected by iOS:

  • Encrypted by iOS automatically
  • Protected by your iPhone passcode/Face ID/Touch ID
  • Only you can access it

Anonymous Device Data (Supabase)

  • Stored on EU Supabase servers
  • HTTPS encryption in transit
  • Encrypted at rest
  • Row Level Security (RLS) enabled

Subscription Data

  • Stored securely on Apple servers
  • HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • We follow Apple's security guidelines

We have no way to access your local meal data.
Only you can see your dishes and plans.

5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you're in the EU or California, you have rights:

Right to Access

Request what data we hold about you.
Reality: We hold anonymous device data (no personal info) and, if subscribed, your email and subscription status.

Right to Deletion

Request deletion of your data.
How: Email alwin.moceri@gmail.com with "Delete My Data"
We will delete your anonymous device data from Supabase within 30 days.

Right to Portability

Request your data in a portable format.
How: Email alwin.moceri@gmail.com

Right to Opt-Out

The anonymous device data collection is necessary for fair free trial enforcement and cannot be opted out of while using the free version. Subscribing to Listie+ removes the need for this enforcement mechanism.

We don't request "App Tracking Transparency" permission.
We collect only the minimum anonymous technical data required to protect our free trial system fairly. We do not track you for advertising or profiling purposes. Period.
