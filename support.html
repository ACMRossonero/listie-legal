Getting Started

Q: What's a "dish"?

Anything you eat! Home-cooked meals, takeout spots, restaurants, or meal prep. Name it however you want: "Pasta Carbonara", "Chicken from bistro", "McDonald's".

For example if you use to eat McDonald's each Wednesday or a meal that does not require any ingredients you can just create a meal called McDonald's.

Then, when you create your week meal plan you can replace the Wednesday meal selected by Listie by the McDonald's meal and that's it.

Q: Do I need internet to use Listie?

No. Listie works 100% offline. Your data is stored locally on your device.

Using Listie

Q: How do I add a dish?

Dishes tab â†’ Tap "+" button â†’ Fill in the details â†’ Save.

Q: What are ingredients for?

Ingredients automatically generate your grocery list. Add them if you want an automatic list, or leave blank if you just want the meal plan.

Q: Can I add recipe links?

Yes! When creating a dish, there's an optional "Recipe URL" field. The link will appear in your meal plan for quick access.

Q: How does anti-repetition work?

Enable it in the "List" tab settings. Listie will avoid dishes from your previous week when generating the new week.

Q: Can I modify a confirmed meal plan?

Yes! Go to the "List" tab â†’ Use one of the three buttons inside individual meals â†’ The "grocery" and "current week" tabs updates automatically.

Grocery List

Q: How is the grocery list created?

Listie automatically compiles ingredients from all dishes in your confirmed meal plan, organized by category.

Q: Can I add items manually?

Yes! In the "Grocery" tab, tap "Add an article" to add non-meal items (e.g., toilet paper, coffee).

Q: Do manual items disappear when I create a new week?

No. Manual items persist across weeks. Check them off, uncheck, or delete as needed.

Data & Privacy

Q: Where is my data stored?

Locally on your iPhone. Listie does NOT upload your meals, plans, or grocery lists to the cloud.

Q: What happens if I delete the app?

If you have iCloud backup enabled:

- Your data is automatically backed up to iCloud

- Reinstalling the app restores your dishes, plans, and settings

If you don't have iCloud backup:

Your data will be lost when you delete the app

Your subscription always remains active (managed by Apple).

Q: How do I enable iCloud backup?

iCloud backup is enabled by default if you have iCloud activated on your iPhone.

To check:

Q: Does Listie track me?

No. We don't track you across apps or websites. See our Privacy Policy for details.
